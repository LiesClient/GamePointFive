Gameplay

There are 4 stats: VIT, SPD, FRT, and DMG

VIT (Vitality) increases max health

SPD (Speed) increases the speed of the character

FRT (Firerate) increases how fast you can attack

DMG (Damage) increases how much damage you do

⠀

You can get XP by killing enemies that can harm you

Get enough XP and you level up, get 1 stat point, and go to max HP

Your score starts at 0, and your score goes up by surviving for longer

To move, use W/A/S/D, and you can aim and shoot with your mouse

You can upgrade stats with 1/2/3/4 to upgrade VIT/SPD/FRT/DMG

Green bar is XP, red bar is HP

There are several enemies, however I won't explain them - you have to figure them out.

Good Luck!
